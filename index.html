About Us

Agra's Most Trusted Jewellery House

Sona Jewels was founded in 1975 in the heart of Agra. Over 48+ years, we have made thousands of families' special moments even more memorable — with the radiance of gold.

We offer BIS Hallmark certified 22K and 24K gold, a unique fusion of diamond and kundan jewellery, honouring Agra's rich heritage while meeting the needs of today's modern family.
